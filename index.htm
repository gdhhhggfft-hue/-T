Welcome to 4T

Thanks for joining our community. This page covers everything you need to know to get started — most importantly, the rules everyone is expected to follow. Breaking these rules can result in warnings, mutes, or bans depending on severity.

⚡

Server Rules

1

No Inappropriate Language

Keep profanity to a minimum. Derogatory language toward any user is prohibited — this includes flirting or matchmaking. This server isn't a dating app.

2

No Spamming

Don't send a lot of small messages right after each other. Do not disrupt chat by spamming.

3

No Pornographic / Adult / Other NSFW Material

This is a community server and not meant to share this kind of material.

4

No Advertisements

We do not tolerate any kind of advertisements, whether it be for other communities or streams.

5

No Server Raiding

Raiding or mentions of raiding are not allowed.

6

⚠ Direct and Indirect Threats

Threats are STRICTLY prohibited. This also includes suicidal threats towards yourself and others. No threats will be tolerated and it will be an immediate ban.

7

Don't Share Personal Information

Do not share your personal information or the personal information of other users without their consent. This includes phone numbers, addresses, and any other sensitive information.

8

Proper Use of Voice and Text Channels

Use channels for their intended purpose only. For example, sending photos in the media and screenshots channel. Avoid disrupting voice chats and be mindful of others when speaking or sharing content.

9

Be Respectful

You must respect all users, regardless of your liking towards them. Treat others the way you want to be treated.
